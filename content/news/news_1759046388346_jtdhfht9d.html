Laure M. Hiendl
Donaueschinger Musiktage 2025 Premiere

A newly commissioned orchestral work, shaped by digital methods and late-Romantic archival sampling, comes to life at the Baarsporthalle, Donaueschingen [1].

Voices Unbound — Festival 2025

From 16–19 October, the festival hosts 23 world premieres and first performances, spanning installations, concerts, and spatial works [2]. Hiendl’s orchestral premiere takes place on Sunday, 19 October at 17:00 [2].

“Applying digital methods to orchestral material, sampling the past and reimagining it spatially.” [2]

Score design, typesetting, and orchestral material by ZSCORE.studio
