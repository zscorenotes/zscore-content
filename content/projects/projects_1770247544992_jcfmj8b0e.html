Kriat Shema

Fantasy for Shofar and Orchestra
Amir Shpilman

Kriat Shema is a fantasy for Shofar and orchestra by Amir Shpilman, premiered at the Berlin Philharmonie. ZSCORE.studio was responsible for the complete editorial preparation of the work, including full score engraving, conductor score, orchestral parts, and performance materials for the premiere.

Editorial Scope

  • Full orchestral score engraving
  • Conductor score preparation
  • Individual orchestral parts
  • Performance-oriented layout optimization
  • Print preparation and material delivery

Premiere Context

Berlin Philharmonie, 2025
